Welcome to CRINGEorBASED. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data to comply with App Store and Google Play guidelines.

We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or contacts. We use Anonymous Authentication and collect your Device ID. This is stored securely in our database solely for:

  • Enforcing daily generation limits.
  • Tracking terms violations (NSFW/Violence attempts).

  • Cloudinary: Used to compile the final shareable videos/GIFs.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure backend infrastructure, database (Firestore), and crash reporting.
