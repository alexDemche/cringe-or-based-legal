Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Welcome to CRINGEorBASED. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data to comply with App Store and Google Play guidelines.

1. Data We Collect (Device IDs)

We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or contacts. We use Anonymous Authentication and collect your Device ID. This is stored securely in our database solely for:

  • Enforcing daily generation limits.
  • Tracking terms violations (NSFW/Violence attempts).
  • Permanently banning abusive devices to maintain a safe environment.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide our core features, we share strictly necessary data with trusted third-party APIs:

  • OpenAI: Your text prompts are sent to OpenAI for moderation and story generation. OpenAI does not use this data to train public models.
  • Giphy: We use Giphy's API to fetch relevant GIFs based on generated keywords.
  • Cloudinary: Used to compile the final shareable videos/GIFs.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure backend infrastructure, database (Firestore), and crash reporting.

3. Advertising & Analytics

Our app offers rewarded ad viewing in exchange for in-game attempts. We use third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may collect and process data like your IP address and device Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings.

4. Age Restriction (Children's Privacy)

This app is intended for mature audiences due to satirical and absurd humor. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or 16 in certain EU countries). If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with data, contact us for immediate deletion.

5. Private Content Generation (Not a Social Network)

CRINGEorBASED is a single-player experience. The stories and videos you generate are created locally for your device. We do not host a public feed, public profiles, or a social timeline. You are solely responsible for any content you choose to export and share externally on third-party social media platforms.

6. Contact Us
